What if the very heart of your nation's most secret defense had been compromised from within? George Smiley, a recently ousted spymaster, is clandestinely recalled to unmask a Soviet mole burrowed deep into British intelligence. His quiet, methodical investigation meticulously peels back layers of Cold War betrayal, where old loyalties clash with deadly new deceptions. The silent hunt for this elusive phantom turns trust into a weapon, threatening to dismantle the very foundations of the Circus.
