Unfolds beneath a scorching Australian sun, a summer destined to reshape a rebellious American teen's world as she's thrust into a vibrant surf culture. From the moment she steps onto the sun-drenched shores, unexpected friendships ignite and cultural clashes spark, forcing her to confront hidden truths. Each crashing wave and golden sunset peels back layers of her guarded exterior, revealing deep-seated insecurities and pushing her limits. This isn't just a holiday; it's a profound journey of self-discovery where survival means finding your true north amidst the chaos.
