Navigating the murky waters of Swampwood, Bill the duck lives a peculiar life as the best friend of Aldo, a gentle giant alligator. Their daily routines are a vibrant tightrope walk, balancing shared laughter with the ever-present, humorous threat of Bill becoming a snack. This animated comedy brilliantly explores the heartwarming absurdity of unlikely companionship and overcoming natural instincts with genuine affection.
