Fueling their entire city, monsters harvest screams from human children, believing them to be dangerously toxic. Suddenly, an innocent human girl, Boo, slips into their frightening realm, shattering every preconceived notion. Gentle giant Sulley must then protect her from the sinister Randall, discovering Boo's infectious laughter holds the true power to transform their world forever.
