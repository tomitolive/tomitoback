Laras life is a slow-moving symphony, a young girl living in a world of silence. She possesses a remarkable artistic talent and turns to music as a means of self-expression. However, she faces betrayal from someone close, drastically altering her lifes path. Lara is torn between self-trust and the betrayal of others, wondering if hidden alliances are trying to silence her.
