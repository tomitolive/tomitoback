هاكسو ريدج Hacksaw Ridge

In the midst of World War II, a US Army medic finds himself at a crossroads: to kill or to save. He refuses to take lives and takes a courageous stand, becoming the first conscientious objector in American history to receive the highest honor. He finds himself in the Battle of Okinawa, where he must face challenges and choose between duty and conscience.
