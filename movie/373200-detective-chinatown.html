محقق الحي الصيني Detective Chinatown

A refined man, rejected from police college, travels to Bangkok where he must team up with a distant, energetic relative to solve a murder case. As they navigate the bustling streets of Bangkok, they encounter comedic situations and challenges at every turn, all while attempting to uncover the truth behind the mysterious killing.
