As the story unfolds, the villainous Emperor Commodus emerges, a ruthless ruler who wreaks havoc on Rome. With a heart devoid of mercy, he murders Maximus' family and enslaves the great general. Maximus finds himself in the gladiatorial arena, transforming into a fierce warrior driven by a bloody path of vengeance against Commodus, the tyrant who shattered his life.
